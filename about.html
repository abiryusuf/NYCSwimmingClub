Welcome and thank you for your interest in NYC Swim Club. NYC Swim Club offers busy professionals a first-class health club experience. NYC Club has developed a non-intimidating, personalized atmosphere where you’ll feel comfortable and confident, not self-conscious. Our facilities and Employee Partners provide the tools and service you need to reach your goals.

New Members and beginners should contact their membership directors for assistance in selecting appropriate classes at our NYC swimming club. Getting in shape doesn’t happen overnight and we want to help you get there safely.
